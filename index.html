Round Glute Shape
→
Most people neglect side glutes — the ones that create the round, curved shape and hip width.
→
Fire Hydrants and lateral moves directly hit the gluteus medius for that hip pop.
→
Frog Pumps target the inner + lower glute for fullness from every angle.
📐
The V-Shape Illusion
→
Building your back and shoulders creates a wider upper frame.
→
This makes your waist appear naturally slimmer even without losing fat there.
→
Core work without obliques keeps your waist tight and neat, not blocky.
